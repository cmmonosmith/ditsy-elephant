Section 1........Usage Instructions:

to play: 
a. send the files "degame.89z" and "degame.89y" to your calculator
b. on the HOME screen, type "degame()" and press the [ENTER] button
c. enjoy!

while playing:
->   LEFT button                  ->        move your card selector left
->   RIGHT button                 ->        move your card selector right
->   [2nd] or [F1] button         ->        select/deselect card at selector
->   SHIFT, [<>], ENTER, ENTER1
     or ENTER2 button             ->        finish selections and play selected cards,
                                            if any if none, pass on your turn
->   [ESC] button                 ->        exit the game completely at almost any time
other prompts in the game will tell you what to press to get a prompted result
